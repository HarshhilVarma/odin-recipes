Ingredients

  • Stock
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • White wine
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat the stock to simmer
  2. Cook the shallots and garlic in the oil
  3. Add the salt, pepper and wine
  4. Add rice and stir till fully coated
  5. Add the hot stock once the wine evaporates
  6. Keep adding the stock until rice is fully cooked
  7. once the rice is done take off heat and stirin cheese until it melts
