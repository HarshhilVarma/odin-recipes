Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are a beloved classic treat featuring a delightful balance of crispy edges and a soft, chewy center. Studded with rich, melty chocolate chips, each bite offers a perfect blend of sweetness and a hint of vanilla. These cookies are a timeless favorite, perfect for any occasion, from casual snacks to festive gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • salt
  • baking soda
  • Vanilla Essence

Instructions

  1. Cream the butter and sugar together
  2. beat in the egg,vanilla,baking soda and salt
  3. Mix in the flour and chocolate chips
  4. Bake at 175 Celsius until golden brown
  5. Let cool and enjoy
