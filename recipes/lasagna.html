Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish renowned for its hearty, layered composition and rich, comforting flavors. It typically consists of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with layers of savory fillings, such as a robust meat sauce made from ground beef or sausage, a creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese mixture, and a tangy marinara or tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat:
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Italian spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Ricotta
  • Egg

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer as follows:
    1. Sauce
    2. noodles
    3. Ricotta
    4. Cheese
    5. Sauce
    6. Repeat the layers, and then top with remaining cheese
  4. Bake until cheese is browned and bubbly
